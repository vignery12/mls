About Us

About Us:

Merit Legal Services was established by La Wanda Ford in 2014.

Merit Legal Services specializes in legal document preparation. We are licensed and bonded Legal Document and Unlawful Detainer Assistants (LDA #2016056276, UDA #2016056278). Ms. Ford is also a Registered Process Server and Certified Mediator. We are not attorneys, a law firm nor a law office and cannot perform the duties of a State Bar Licensed Attorney. We are an effective and inexpensive alternative.

Ms. Ford has 38+ years of experience in legal document preparation. Ms. Ford would like to make available to you her many years of experience and high level of professionalism.

We are experienced in legal document preparation in the following areas of law: Small Claims, Unlawful Detainer (Evictions), Security Deposit Refunds, Divorce, Separation, Annulment, Spousal Support, Child Custody, Child Support, Visitation, Establishing Paternity, Name Change, Restraining Orders, Conservatorship, Guardianship, Retirement QDRO, Last Will and Testament, Living Trust, Power of Attorney, Advance Healthcare Directives, Business Incorporation, Mediation and other professional services.
